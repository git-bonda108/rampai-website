AI for All

Understanding and Embracing Artificial Intelligence in the Workplace

Duration

1.5-2 Days (12-16 hours)

Target

All employees - technical and non-technical

Level

Foundational

Comprehensive foundational training designed for employees across all functions who wish to gain practical understanding of AI and its workplace applications.

Prerequisites

  • No prior AI experience required

  • Openness to learn new technology concepts
